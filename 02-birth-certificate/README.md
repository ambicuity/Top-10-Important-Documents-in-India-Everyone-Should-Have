# Birth Certificate

A birth certificate is a vital document issued by the hospital or municipal authorities on the day a child is born. This document confirms you were born on a specific date, day, time, at a specific hospital, and country.

## What is a Birth Certificate?

A birth certificate is an official legal document that records the birth of a child. It serves as the first official proof of identity and contains essential information such as:
- Full name of the child
- Date and time of birth
- Place of birth (hospital/home)
- Names of parents
- Registration number
- Date of registration

## Why It Is Important

Your birth certificate is your **first identity proof before you turn 18**. It is one of the most important documents in India for:

### 1. School Admission
Required for enrollment in schools and educational institutions

### 2. Driving Licence Application
Mandatory age proof for obtaining a driving license

### 3. Marriage Registration
Essential document for marriage registration

### 4. Government Jobs
Required for age verification in government job applications

### 5. International Travel
Necessary for obtaining a passport and international travel

### 6. Legal Proceedings
Valid document in legal matters requiring age or identity proof

### 7. Property Rights
Important for claiming inheritance and property rights

### 8. Other Documents
Required for obtaining other important documents like Aadhaar, PAN card, etc.

## Birth Certificate for Newborns

A newborn baby's birth certificate is:
- Automatically filed by the hospital
- Registered with the local municipal corporation or gram panchayat
- Legalized with an affidavit signed by the parents
- Should be obtained within 21 days of birth for free registration

## Birth Certificate for Adults

If you don't have a birth certificate:
- Can apply for a delayed birth certificate
- Requires additional documentation
- May need affidavits from parents or witnesses
- Involves verification process

## Birth Certificate Attestation

An attested birth certificate is mandatory for use in a foreign country. The attestation process includes:
1. Notary attestation
2. Home Department attestation
3. MEA (Ministry of External Affairs) attestation
4. Embassy attestation

**Superb Enterprise Private Limited (SEPL)** is an officially approved agency by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) that helps applicants in the birth certificate attestation procedure.

## Validity

- Birth certificate is a **lifetime document**
- Does not expire
- Should be kept safely
- Original is always required for verification
