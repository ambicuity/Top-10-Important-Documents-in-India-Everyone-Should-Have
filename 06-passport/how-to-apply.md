# How to Apply for Passport

## Application Methods

1. **Online Application** (Recommended)
2. **Offline Application** (At Passport Seva Kendra)

## Method 1: Online Application (Recommended)

### Step 1: Register on Passport Seva Portal

**Website**: [https://www.passportindia.gov.in](https://www.passportindia.gov.in)

- Click on "New User? Register Now"
- Select user type (Applicant, Diplomatic, Official)
- Fill registration details:
  - Given name
  - Date of birth
  - Email ID
  - Login ID
  - Password
  - Security question
- Verify email address

### Step 2: Login to Portal

- Use registered login ID and password
- Complete security verification

### Step 3: Fill Application Form

- Click on "Apply for Fresh Passport/Reissue of Passport"
- Select appropriate service:
  - Fresh Passport
  - Reissue of Passport
  - Additional Booklet
- Fill form with accurate details:
  - Personal information
  - Family details
  - Present and permanent address
  - Emergency contact
  - Previous passport details (if reissue)
- Upload photograph (35mm x 45mm, JPEG format)
- Save and continue

### Step 4: Pay Fees

**Fee Structure:**
- Fresh Passport (36 pages) - 10 years validity: ₹1,500
- Fresh Passport (60 pages) - 10 years validity: ₹2,000
- Minor passport (under 18) - 5 years validity: ₹1,000
- Tatkal service: Additional ₹2,000

**Payment Methods:**
- Credit/Debit card
- Internet banking
- SBI Challan

### Step 5: Book Appointment

- View available slots at nearest Passport Seva Kendra (PSK)
- Select convenient date and time
- Print application receipt and appointment confirmation

### Step 6: Visit Passport Seva Kendra

**Documents to Carry:**
- Printed application form
- Appointment confirmation
- Original documents (as per requirements list)
- Self-attested photocopies

**At PSK:**
- Token number will be issued
- Biometric data capture (fingerprints, photo)
- Document verification
- Brief interview
- Collect acknowledgment slip

### Step 7: Police Verification

- Post-verification (normal) or Pre-verification (Tatkal)
- Police will visit your address
- Verification of address and antecedents
- Completed within 3-4 weeks

### Step 8: Passport Printing and Dispatch

- After police clearance
- Passport printed within 2-3 days
- Dispatched via Speed Post
- Track using file number

**Total Time:**
- Normal: 30-45 days
- Tatkal: 7-10 days (for major cities)

## Required Documents

### Identity Proof (Any ONE):
- Aadhaar Card
- Voter ID
- PAN Card
- Driving License
- Government ID

### Address Proof (Any ONE):
- Aadhaar Card
- Electricity Bill
- Water Bill
- Bank Statement
- Rent Agreement

### Date of Birth Proof (Any ONE):
- Birth Certificate
- 10th Certificate
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card

**For complete list, see**: [Required Documents](./required-documents.md)
