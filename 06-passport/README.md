# Passport

A passport is one of the most important documents you need to travel to foreign countries. This document serves as a license that proves your identity and nationality in a foreign country.

## What is a Passport?

A passport is an official travel document issued by the Government of India through the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA). It certifies the identity and nationality of the holder for international travel.

## Types of Indian Passports

### 1. Regular Passport (Blue)
- For ordinary citizens
- Used for tourism, business, education, and employment
- Validity: 10 years (for adults), 5 years (for minors)

### 2. Official Passport (White)
- For government officials traveling on official duty
- Valid only for official purposes

### 3. Diplomatic Passport (Maroon)
- For Indian diplomats and senior government officials
- Provides diplomatic immunity

## Did You Know?

Indian passport holders have the power to enter **60+ foreign countries without a visa**! This includes popular destinations like:
- Thailand
- Mauritius
- Maldives
- Indonesia
- Serbia
- And many more

## Why You Need a Passport

### 1. International Travel
- Mandatory for traveling outside India
- Entry requirement for all countries
- Required for visa applications

### 2. Proof of Citizenship
- Valid proof of Indian nationality
- Accepted worldwide
- Contains nationality details

### 3. Identity Document
- Government-issued photo ID
- Contains biometric information
- Universally accepted identification

### 4. Study Abroad
- Required for student visa applications
- Mandatory for university admissions abroad
- Needed for scholarship applications

### 5. Foreign Employment
- Essential for work visas
- Required for employment contracts
- Needed for relocation

### 6. Immigration and Settlement
- Mandatory for permanent residency applications
- Required for citizenship applications in other countries
- Needed for migration processes

## Passport Details

A standard passport contains:
- Holder's photograph
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Place of birth
- Gender
- Passport number
- Date of issue
- Date of expiry
- Signature
- Address
- Emergency contact information

## Validity

- **For adults (18+ years)**: 10 years
- **For minors (below 18)**: 5 years
- Can be renewed before expiry
- Should have minimum 6 months validity for international travel
