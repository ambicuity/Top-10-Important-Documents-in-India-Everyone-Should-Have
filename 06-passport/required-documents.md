# Required Documents for Passport

## Essential Documents

### 1. Proof of Identity (Any ONE)
- Aadhaar Card
- Voter ID Card
- PAN Card
- Driving License
- Government Photo ID Card
- Pensioner Card
- Bank Passbook with photograph

### 2. Proof of Address (Any ONE)
- Aadhaar Card
- Voter ID Card
- Electricity Bill (not older than 3 months)
- Water Bill (not older than 3 months)
- Bank Statement (not older than 3 months)
- Rent Agreement (registered)
- Property Tax Receipt
- Telephone Bill - Landline

### 3. Date of Birth Proof (Any ONE)
- Birth Certificate
- 10th Class Certificate
- 12th Class Certificate
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card
- Driving License

## Additional Documents

### For Parents
- Marriage certificate
- Spouse passport copy (first and last pages)

### For Address Proof
- Running bank account's passbook photo
- House electricity bill
- Rent agreement
- Gas connection proof
- Postpaid mobile bill

### For Employment
- Certificate from employer on company letterhead
- Income tax assessment order copy

### For Students
- School leaving certificate copy
- Student ID card

### For Insurance
- Policy Bond copy issued by Public Life Insurance

## Special Requirements

### For Minors (Under 18)
- Birth certificate (mandatory)
- Parents' passports (first and last pages)
- Parents' Aadhaar cards
- Annexure D (consent affidavit from parents)

### For Tatkal Application
- All above documents
- Proof of urgency (if any)
- Additional fees

## Document Format

- Clear photocopies
- Self-attested
- Original for verification
- Recent (as specified)
