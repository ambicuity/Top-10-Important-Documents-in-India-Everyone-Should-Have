# How to Apply for Driving Licence

## Two-Step Process

1. **Apply for Learner's Licence (LL)**
2. **Apply for Permanent Driving Licence (DL)**

## Step 1: Apply for Learner's Licence

### Online Application

**Website**: [https://parivahan.gov.in/parivahan](https://parivahan.gov.in/parivahan) or state RTO website

#### Process:

**1. Register on Portal**
- Select your state
- Click on "Online Services"
- Select "Driving Licence Related Services"
- Choose "Apply for Learner's Licence"

**2. Fill Application Form**
- Personal details
- Address information
- Type of vehicle (MC WG, LMV, etc.)
- Upload photograph and signature

**3. Book Slot for Test**
- Select RTO office
- Choose date and time
- Pay application fee (₹200-₹400)

**4. Visit RTO on Scheduled Date**

**Documents to Carry:**
- Application form printout
- Age proof (Birth certificate, 10th certificate, Aadhaar)
- Address proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, etc.)
- Passport-size photographs (4-6 copies)
- Medical certificate (if applicable)

**5. Biometric and Photo Capture**
- At RTO counter
- Fingerprints and photograph

**6. Take Written Test**
- Computer-based test
- Multiple choice questions
- Traffic rules and road signs
- Pass mark: 60% (12 out of 20 questions)
- Available in multiple languages

**7. Collect Learner's Licence**
- If test passed, LL issued same day
- Valid for 6 months
- Can drive with 'L' sign and experienced driver

### Fees
- Application: ₹150-₹200
- Test fees: ₹50
- Smart card: ₹200
- **Total**: ₹400-₹450

## Step 2: Apply for Permanent Driving Licence

### Eligibility
- Must hold valid Learner's Licence
- Minimum 30 days of practice with LL
- Within 6 months of LL issuance

### Online Application

**1. Login to Portal**
- Same portal as LL application
- Select "Apply for Driving Licence"

**2. Enter LL Number**
- Provide Learner's Licence details
- Verify information

**3. Book Slot for Driving Test**
- Select RTO and date
- Pay fees (₹300-₹500)

**4. Visit RTO on Test Date**

**Documents Required:**
- Learner's Licence (original)
- Application form
- All documents submitted for LL
- Test fees receipt

**5. Driving Test**
- Practical driving test on RTO track
- Following instructor's directions
- Testing vehicle handling skills
- Duration: 10-15 minutes

**Test Includes:**
- Starting and stopping vehicle
- Reverse parking
- Figure-8 driving
- Traffic rules adherence
- Road signs recognition

**6. Collect Permanent Driving Licence**
- If test passed
- Smart card DL issued within 7-30 days
- Sent by post or collect from RTO
- Valid for 20 years (until age 50)

### Fees
- Application: ₹200
- Smart card: ₹200
- Test fees: ₹300
- **Total**: ₹700-₹800

## Total Process Timeline

- **Learner's Licence**: 1 day (test day)
- **Practice Period**: Minimum 30 days
- **Permanent DL**: 7-30 days after test

## Offline Application (Without Online Booking)

1. Visit RTO office directly
2. Fill Form 2 (for LL) or Form 4 (for DL)
3. Submit documents at counter
4. Pay fees
5. Take test same day (if slots available)

**Note**: Online booking recommended to avoid long queues

## Track Application Status

**Website**: State RTO portal
**Required**: Application number or LL/DL number

## Tips for Tests

### For Written Test (LL)
✅ Study traffic rules manual
✅ Practice online mock tests
✅ Learn road signs
✅ Take test in comfortable language
✅ Don't rush, read questions carefully

### For Driving Test (DL)
✅ Practice extensively with LL
✅ Know vehicle controls well
✅ Follow test instructor's directions
✅ Drive at moderate speed
✅ Check mirrors regularly
✅ Use indicators properly
✅ Don't panic if you make small mistakes

## Common Reasons for Test Failure

### Written Test
❌ Not studying traffic rules
❌ Rushing through questions
❌ Not understanding road signs

### Driving Test
❌ Not following instructions
❌ Rash driving
❌ Not using indicators
❌ Poor vehicle control
❌ Hitting track boundaries
❌ Not checking mirrors

## Retest Policy

- **LL Test**: Can reattempt after 7 days
- **DL Test**: Can reattempt after 7 days
- Additional fees for each attempt
- No limit on number of attempts

## International Driving Permit (IDP)

If planning to drive abroad:
- Apply for IDP after getting Indian DL
- Valid for 1 year
- Application at RTO
- Fees: ₹1,000
- Required documents: DL, passport, visa copy
