# Driving Licence

In India, anyone who drives and is above 18 can apply for a driving license. Drivers must always carry their driving license while operating their vehicle for use in case of police investigation, accident, etc.

## What is a Driving Licence?

A driving license is an official document issued by the Regional Transport Office (RTO) that authorizes the holder to operate motorvehicles on public roads in India. It certifies that the holder has met the required standards for safe driving.

## Types of Driving Licences

### 1. Learner's Licence (LL)
- Valid for 6 months
- Must display 'L' sign on vehicle
- Cannot drive alone (experienced driver must accompany)
- First step before permanent license

### 2. Permanent Driving Licence (DL)
- Valid for 20 years (until age 50) or lifetime
- Allows independent driving
- Obtained after passing driving test

## Vehicle Categories

- **MC 50CC**: Motorcycles without gear (up to 50cc)
- **MC WG**: Motorcycles with gear
- **LMV**: Light Motor Vehicle (cars, jeeps)
- **HMV**: Heavy Motor Vehicle (trucks, buses)
- **Transport Vehicle**: Commercial vehicles
- **Others**: Special category vehicles

## Minimum Age Requirements

- **Motorcycles without gear (MC 50CC)**: 16 years (with parental consent)
- **Motorcycles with gear (MC WG)**: 18 years
- **Light Motor Vehicle (LMV)**: 18 years
- **Commercial Vehicle**: 20 years

## Why You Need a Driving Licence

### 1. Legal Requirement
- Mandatory to drive on public roads
- Violation attracts penalties and fines
- Legal document for vehicle operation

### 2. Identity Proof
- Valid government-issued photo ID
- Contains address and DOB
- Widely accepted identification

### 3. Age Proof
- Accepted as date of birth proof
- Valid for various applications
- Official age verification document

### 4. Insurance Claims
- Required for vehicle insurance claims
- Necessary for accident compensation
- Insurance validity linked to valid license

### 5. Renting Vehicles
- Mandatory for car/bike rentals
- Required for commercial vehicle hire
- Essential for vehicle leasing
