# Required Documents for Driving Licence

## For Learner's Licence (LL)

### Age Proof (Any ONE)
- Birth Certificate
- PAN Card
- Passport
- 10th Class Mark Sheet
- 12th Class Mark Sheet
- School Leaving Certificate
- Aadhaar Card
- Matriculation Certificate

### Address Proof (Any ONE)
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport
- Voter ID Card
- Bank Statement (not older than 3 months)
- Electricity Bill (not older than 2 months)
- Water Bill (not older than 2 months)
- Telephone Bill (not older than 2 months)
- Rent Agreement (registered)
- Ration Card
- Self-owned house agreement

### Identity Proof (Any ONE)
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport
- Voter ID Card
- PAN Card
- Ration Card with photograph
- Bank passbook with photograph
- Government issued photo ID

### Additional Documents
- Passport-size photographs (4-6 copies, white background)
- Form 2 (Learner's Licence application form)
- Medical certificate (for commercial vehicle licence)
- Form 1A (Medical certificate) if above 40 years

### For Minors (16-18 years)
- Parental consent form
- Parent's identity proof
- School ID card

## For Permanent Driving Licence (DL)

### Mandatory Documents
- Valid Learner's Licence (original)
- Application Form 4
- All documents submitted for LL (photocopies)
- Passport-size photographs (3-4 copies)

### If Applying at Different RTO
- Address proof of new location
- Transfer application

### For Commercial Vehicle Licence
- Medical certificate (Form 1A)
- Fitness certificate
- Higher age requirement proof (20+ years)

### For Renewal of DL
- Existing driving licence
- Application Form 9
- Medical certificate (if above 40 years)
- Address proof (if changed)
- Passport-size photographs

## Document Format Requirements

### Photographs
- Size: 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm
- White background
- Recent (not older than 3 months)
- Full face, front view
- No sunglasses

### Photocopies
- Clear and legible
- Self-attested
- Original for verification

## State-Specific Requirements

Different states may have slight variations. Check your state RTO website for exact requirements.

### Common Variations
- Some states require domicile certificate
- Medical certificate requirements vary
- Photograph specifications may differ

## Medical Certificate Requirements

### When Required
- All commercial vehicle licences
- All applicants above 40 years for renewal
- Heavy motor vehicle licence
- Transport vehicle licence

### Where to Obtain
- Government hospitals
- RTO-authorized medical practitioners
- Primary Health Centers

### Validity
- 1 year from issue date
- Must be valid on application date

## Document Checklist

### For Learner's Licence
- [ ] Age proof (original + photocopy)
- [ ] Address proof (original + photocopy)
- [ ] Identity proof (original + photocopy)
- [ ] 4-6 passport-size photographs
- [ ] Form 2 (filled and signed)
- [ ] Medical certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] Fees paid receipt

### For Permanent DL
- [ ] Learner's Licence (original)
- [ ] Application Form 4
- [ ] Previous document copies
- [ ] 3-4 passport-size photographs
- [ ] Fees paid receipt
- [ ] Medical certificate (if applicable)
