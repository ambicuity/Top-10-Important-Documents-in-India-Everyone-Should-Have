# How to Apply for Marriage Certificate

## Methods to Apply

1. **Online Application** (available in most states)
2. **Offline Application** (at Registrar office)

## Online Application Process

### Step 1: Visit State Portal

Each state has its own marriage registration portal. Examples:
- **Delhi**: [https://dgsnd.delhi.gov.in](https://dgsnd.delhi.gov.in)
- **Maharashtra**: [https://aaplesarkar.mahaonline.gov.in](https://aaplesarkar.mahaonline.gov.in)
- **Karnataka**: [https://nadakacheri.karnataka.gov.in](https://nadakacheri.karnataka.gov.in)
- **Tamil Nadu**: [https://www.tnreginet.net](https://www.tnreginet.net)

### Step 2: Select Marriage Registration

- Find "Marriage Registration" service
- Select "Apply for Marriage Registration"

### Step 3: Fill Application Form

**Bride's Details**:
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Address
- Father's name
- Mother's name
- Marital status

**Groom's Details**:
- Same as above

**Marriage Details**:
- Date of marriage
- Place of marriage
- Type of marriage (Hindu/Special Marriage Act/etc.)
- Witnesses details (2-3 witnesses)

### Step 4: Upload Documents

- Marriage certificate application form
- Passport-size photos (bride and groom)
- Age proof (both)
- Address proof (both)
- Marriage invitation card
- Marriage photos
- Affidavits (if required)
- Divorce decree (for divorcees)
- Death certificate (for widows/widowers)

### Step 5: Pay Fees

- Hindu Marriage Act: ₹100
- Special Marriage Act: ₹150
- Payment via online mode

### Step 6: Book Appointment

- Select date for document verification
- Choose convenient time slot
- At Registrar office

### Step 7: Visit Registrar Office

- Both spouses must be present
- Carry all original documents
- Witnesses may need to be present
- Officer will verify documents

### Step 8: Receive Certificate

- Certificate issued within 7-30 days
- Can download online or collect from office

## Offline Application Process

### Step 1: Visit Registrar Office

- Sub-Registrar office of your area
- Marriage Officer office

### Step 2: Obtain Application Form

- Collect form from counter
- Or download and print beforehand

### Step 3: Fill Form

- Fill in capital letters
- Provide all mandatory details
- Both spouses must sign

### Step 4: Submit Documents

**Required Documents** (attach photocopies):
- Marriage certificate application form (duly filled)
- Passport-size photos (bride and groom - 3 each)
- Age proofs (10th certificate/birth certificate/Aadhaar)
- Address proofs (Aadhaar/Voter ID/passport)
- Marriage invitation card
- Marriage photos (1-2 photos)
- Affidavits (from both spouses)
- Witness details (2-3 witnesses with ID proofs)

**For Divorcees**:
- Divorce certificate or decree

**For Widow/Widower**:
- Death certificate of deceased spouse

### Step 5: Pay Fees

- At cashier counter
- Get receipt

### Step 6: Verification

- Officer verifies documents
- May ask questions
- Witnesses may be called

### Step 7: Registration

- Marriage registered in register
- Entry number provided

### Step 8: Collect Certificate

- Certificate issued within 7-30 days
- Collect from same office

## Timeline

- **Application to Verification**: 7-15 days
- **Verification to Certificate**: 7-15 days
- **Total Time**: 15-30 days (varies by state)

## Fees

| Act | Registration Fee |
|-----|------------------|
| Hindu Marriage Act | ₹100 |
| Special Marriage Act | ₹150 |
| Additional certified copies | ₹10-₹50 per copy |

## After Marriage - When to Register

**Best Practice**: Register within 30 days of marriage
**Late Registration**: Can register anytime after marriage (may require additional affidavits)

## For Marriages Conducted Abroad

- Register with Indian Embassy/Consulate
- Or register in India with marriage proof from abroad
- Additional documents required

## Track Application Status

- Check on state portal with reference number
- Call Registrar office
- Visit in person

## Tips

✅ Apply early (don't wait until you need it)
✅ Get multiple certified copies
✅ Keep originals safe
✅ Both spouses should be present
✅ Arrange witnesses in advance
✅ Keep marriage photos and invitation safe
