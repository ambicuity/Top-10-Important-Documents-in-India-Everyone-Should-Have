# Required Documents for Marriage Certificate

## Essential Documents for Both Bride and Groom

### 1. Age Proof (Any ONE for each)
- 10th Class Certificate
- Birth Certificate
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport
- PAN Card
- Driving License

**Minimum Age**: Bride 18 years, Groom 21 years

### 2. Address Proof (Any ONE for each)
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport
- Voter ID
- Driving License
- Ration Card
- Bank Passbook
- Utility bills (electricity/water/gas)

### 3. Identity Proof (Any ONE for each)
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport
- Voter ID
- PAN Card
- Driving License

### 4. Photographs
- Passport-size photos (3-4 for each spouse)
- Marriage photographs (1-2 photos showing couple)
- Recent photos (not older than 3 months)

## Additional Documents

### 5. Marriage Invitation Card
- Original invitation card
- Shows date and venue of marriage

### 6. Affidavits
**Separate affidavits by husband and wife** stating:
- They are unmarried or divorced/widowed
- Consent to marriage
- Date and place of marriage
- Witnesses present

**Format**: On ‚Çπ10 stamp paper, notarized

### 7. Witnesses
**2-3 witnesses required**
- Identity proof of each witness
- Address proof of each witness
- Contact details

### 8. Marriage Certificate Application Form
- Duly filled and signed
- Available at Registrar office or download online

## Special Case Documents

### For Divorcees
- Divorce certificate
- Divorce decree from court
- Affidavit stating divorced status

### For Widow/Widower
- Death certificate of previous spouse
- Affidavit stating widow/widower status

### For Foreign Nationals
- Passport (with valid visa)
- NOC from Embassy
- Certificate of no-impediment
- Passport-size photographs

### For Interfaith Marriage (Special Marriage Act)
- Notice to Registrar (30 days advance)
- All regular documents
- Objection period documents (if any objections raised)

## Document Format

### Photocopies
- Clear and legible
- Self-attested by applicant
- Original for verification

### Photographs
- Size: 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm (passport size)
- White/light background
- Recent (not older than 3 months)
- Both partners together for marriage photo

### Affidavits
- On ‚Çπ10 stamp paper
- Notarized by notary public
- Separate for bride and groom
