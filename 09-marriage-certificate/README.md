# Marriage Certificate

A marriage certificate is proof that two individuals are officially married. This certificate is one of the important documents issued by the Registrar of Marriage in India.

## What is a Marriage Certificate?

A marriage certificate is a legal document that proves a marriage has been solemnized and registered according to Indian law. It contains details of both spouses and the marriage ceremony.

## Why You Need a Marriage Certificate

### 1. Legal Proof of Marriage
- Official recognition of marital status
- Legal validity in courts
- Proof of relationship

### 2. Name Change
- For wife/husband to change surname
- Update in official documents
- Passport application/update

### 3. Visa Applications
- Spouse visa for foreign countries
- Immigration purposes
- Dependent visa applications

### 4. Joint Bank Accounts
- Opening joint accounts
- Adding spouse as nominee
- Joint loans

### 5. Insurance Claims
- Health insurance for spouse
- Life insurance nominations
- Claim settlements

### 6. Property Rights
- Joint property ownership
- Inheritance rights
- Succession matters

### 7. Passport
- Spouse name endorsement
- Address change
- Name change post marriage
