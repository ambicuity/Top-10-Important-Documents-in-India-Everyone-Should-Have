## Instant e-PAN (FREE)

**Generate Instant e-PAN**:
- **Website**: [https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal](https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal)
- **Requirements**: Aadhaar number with linked mobile
- **Fee**: **FREE**
- **Time**: Instant (10-15 minutes)
- **Process**: 
  1. Enter Aadhaar number
  2. Verify with OTP
  3. e-PAN sent to email

## PAN Services

### Link Aadhaar with PAN

**Official Portal**:
- **Website**: [https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/](https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/)
- **Navigate to**: "Link Aadhaar" under "Quick Links"
- **Mandatory**: As per Income Tax Act
- **Free**: No charges

**SMS Method**:
- Send SMS: `UIDPAN <12-digit Aadhaar> <10-digit PAN>` to 567678 or 56161

**Example**: UIDPAN 123456789012 ABCDE1234F

### Verify PAN

**Check PAN Status**:
- **Website**: [https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/](https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/)
- **Purpose**: Verify if PAN is valid and active
- **Process**: Login and check PAN status

**Verify Aadhaar-PAN Link Status**:
- **Website**: [https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/](https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/)
- **Navigate to**: "Link Aadhaar Status"
- **Enter**: PAN and Aadhaar numbers

## Important Reminders

⚠️ **Beware of Frauds**
- Use only official government websites
- NSDL and UTIITSL are authorized agencies
- Don't share PAN details on unsecured websites
- Don't pay unauthorized agents

⚠️ **Mandatory Aadhaar Linking**
- Link PAN with Aadhaar to keep it active
- Unlinked PAN becomes inoperative
- Free service on Income Tax portal

⚠️ **Instant e-PAN is FREE**
- No charges for Aadhaar-based e-PAN
- Beware of websites charging money
