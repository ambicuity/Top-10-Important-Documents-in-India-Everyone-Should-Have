# How to Apply for PAN Card

## Methods to Apply

1. **Online Application** (Recommended - Fast and Easy)
2. **Offline Application** (Through PAN centers)
3. **Instant e-PAN** (Aadhaar-based - FREE and Immediate)

## Method 1: Instant e-PAN (FREE - Recommended)

### For Indian Citizens with Aadhaar

This is the **fastest** and **FREE** method!

**Website**: [https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal](https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal)

### Step-by-Step Process:

**Step 1**: Visit Income Tax e-filing portal

**Step 2**: Click on "Instant e-PAN"

**Step 3**: Enter Aadhaar number

**Step 4**: Verify details pre-filled from Aadhaar:
- Name
- Date of birth
- Gender
- Address

**Step 5**: Receive OTP on Aadhaar-registered mobile number

**Step 6**: Enter OTP to verify

**Step 7**: e-PAN generated instantly and sent to registered email

**Step 8**: Download e-PAN (PDF format)

**Time Taken**: 10-15 minutes
**Fee**: **FREE**
**Validity**: Same as physical PAN card

### Requirements:
- Valid Aadhaar number
- Mobile number linked with Aadhaar
- Email address

## Method 2: Online Application (NSDL/UTIITSL)

### Through NSDL Portal

**Website**: [https://www.onlineservices.nsdl.com/paam/endUserRegisterContact.html](https://www.onlineservices.nsdl.com/paam/endUserRegisterContact.html)

### Step-by-Step Process:

**Step 1**: Visit NSDL PAN application website

**Step 2**: Select application type:
- **Form 49A**: For Indian citizens
- **Form 49AA**: For foreign citizens

**Step 3**: Select category:
- Individual
- Company
- Firm
- HUF
- AOP/BOI
- Trust
- Others

**Step 4**: Fill application form with:
- Personal details (Name, Father's name, Date of birth)
- Contact details (Email, Mobile)
- Address details (Residential and communication)
- Identity documents
- Address proof documents

**Step 5**: Upload documents:
- Identity proof (scanned copy)
- Address proof (scanned copy)
- Date of birth proof (scanned copy)
- Photograph (recent passport size)

**Step 6**: Make payment:
- Indian communication address: ‚Çπ93 (‚Çπ93 + GST = ‚Çπ110)
- Foreign communication address: ‚Çπ864 (‚Çπ864 + GST = ‚Çπ1020)
- Payment modes: Credit card, debit card, net banking, demand draft

**Step 7**: Submit application

**Step 8**: Note acknowledgment number (15-digit)

**Step 9**: Take printout of acknowledgment

**Step 10**: Send documents to NSDL:
- Print and sign acknowledgment
- Attach photograph
- Attach self-attested document copies
- Send to NSDL address (mentioned on acknowledgment)
- Speed post or courier within 15 days

**Step 11**: Track application status using acknowledgment number

**Step 12**: Receive PAN card by post (15-20 days)

### Through UTIITSL Portal

**Website**: [https://www.pan.utiitsl.com/PAN](https://www.pan.utiitsl.com/PAN)

**Process**: Similar to NSDL process

## Method 3: Offline Application

### Visit PAN Service Center

**Step 1**: Find nearest PAN center
- Search on NSDL or UTIITSL website
- Visit the center

**Step 2**: Collect application form
- Form 49A (Indian citizens)
- Form 49AA (Foreign citizens)

**Step 3**: Fill form:
- Use CAPITAL LETTERS
- Fill all mandatory fields
- Don't leave blank spaces (use N/A if not applicable)
- Sign in designated area

**Step 4**: Attach documents:
- 2 passport size photographs
- Identity proof (self-attested photocopy)
- Address proof (self-attested photocopy)
- Date of birth proof (self-attested photocopy)

**Step 5**: Submit form at counter

**Step 6**: Pay fee (cash/card/DD):
- ‚Çπ110 (including GST) for Indian address
- ‚Çπ1020 (including GST) for foreign address

**Step 7**: Get acknowledgment slip

**Step 8**: Track status using acknowledgment number

**Step 9**: Receive PAN card by post (15-30 days)

## For Minors (Below 18 Years)

**Process**: Same as above but with these additions:

**Additional Documents**:
- Parent's PAN card (mandatory)
- Birth certificate of minor
- Guardian's identity proof
- Guardian must sign the application

**Note**: Minor's PAN card will be linked with parent's PAN

## For NRIs (Non-Resident Indians)

**Can Apply**:
- Online through NSDL/UTIITSL
- Through representative in India (with authorization letter)

**Documents Required**:
- Passport (mandatory)
- Foreign address proof
- OCI/PIO card (if applicable)
- Indian address proof (if available)

**Fee**: ‚Çπ1020 (including GST) - foreign dispatch

## Documents Required

### Identity Proof (Any ONE)

- Aadhaar card
- Voter ID card
- Driving license
- Passport
- Ration card
- Arms license
- Photo identity card issued by government
- Pensioner card with photograph
- Central Government Health Service Scheme card

### Address Proof (Any ONE)

- Aadhaar card
- Voter ID card
- Driving license
- Passport
- Bank statement/passbook (not older than 3 months)
- Electricity bill (not older than 3 months)
- Telephone bill - landline (not older than 3 months)
- Ration card
- Property tax receipt
- Credit card statement (not older than 3 months)
- Registered rent/lease agreement
- Employer certificate on letterhead

### Date of Birth Proof (Any ONE)

- Birth certificate
- 10th class certificate (SSLC/SSC)
- Passport
- Matriculation certificate
- Driving license
- Aadhaar card (if DOB mentioned)
- Marriage certificate (if DOB mentioned)

**Note**: Aadhaar card can serve as all three proofs (Identity, Address, DOB)

## PAN Card Delivery

**Delivery Method**: Speed post
**Delivery Time**: 
- Metro cities: 7-10 days after dispatch
- Other cities: 10-15 days after dispatch

**Tracking Speed Post**:
- Use tracking number (mentioned in application status)
- Track on India Post website

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Issue 1: Documents not matching
**Solution**: Ensure name, DOB, and address match across all documents

### Issue 2: Application rejected
**Solution**: Check rejection reason, correct errors, reapply

### Issue 3: PAN not received
**Solution**: 
- Track application status
- Check if PAN was allotted
- Contact NSDL/UTIITSL helpline
- Visit nearest TIN-FC (PAN center)

### Issue 4: Wrong details in PAN
**Solution**: Apply for PAN correction/update
