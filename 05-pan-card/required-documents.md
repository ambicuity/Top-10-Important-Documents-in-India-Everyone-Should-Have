# Required Documents for PAN Card

## Overview

To apply for a PAN card, you need to provide:
- **Identity Proof** - Any ONE document
- **Address Proof** - Any ONE document  
- **Date of Birth Proof** - Any ONE document

**Note**: A single document like Aadhaar Card can serve all three purposes.

## Complete List of Acceptable Documents

### Identity Proof (Any ONE Required)

1. **Aadhaar Card** ‚úÖ (Most preferred - also covers address and DOB)
2. **Voter ID Card** (Election Commission Photo Identity Card)
3. **Driving Licence**
4. **Passport**
5. **Ration Card** with photograph
6. **Arms Licence**
7. **Photo identity card** issued by Central/State Government or PSU
8. **Pensioner Card** with photograph issued by Central/State Government
9. **Central Government Health Service Scheme (CGHS) Card** or Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme (ECHS) Photo Card

12. **School/College Photo Identity Card**
13. **Office identity card** issued by Government or PSU
14. **Spouse's passport** (in case of housewife, photocopy with spouse's PAN copy)

## Documents for Different Categories

### For Individual (Resident Indian)

**Minimum Required:**
- Aadhaar Card (covers identity, address, and DOB)

**OR**

- Identity Proof (one from list)
- Address Proof (one from list)
- DOB Proof (one from list)

**Plus:**
- Recent passport-size photograph (2 copies)

### For Minor (Below 18 Years)

**Required:**
- Parent's/Guardian's PAN card (mandatory)
- Birth certificate of minor
- Guardian's identity proof
- Guardian's address proof
- Guardian's photograph
- Minor's photograph

**Note**: Application must be signed by parent/guardian

### For NRI (Non-Resident Indian)

**Required:**
- Passport (mandatory - copy of first 2 and last 2 pages)
- Foreign address proof:
  - Bank statement from foreign bank
  - Driving license
  - Utility bill
  - Residence card
  - Tax assessment order
- OCI/PIO card (if applicable)
- Indian address proof (if claiming Indian address)

### For Foreign Citizen

**Required:**
- Passport (mandatory - copy with Indian visa)
- Foreign address proof
- Indian address proof (residential/office in India)
- Photograph
- Copy of visa with stay duration proof

### For Hindu Undivided Family (HUF)

**Required:**
- Karta's (head of HUF) PAN card
- HUF deed or declaration
- Karta's identity proof
- HUF address proof
- Karta's photograph
- Bank account details in HUF name

### For Company

**Required:**
- Certificate of Incorporation
- Registered office address proof
- Board resolution for PAN application
- Authorized signatory's identity proof
- MOA and AOA copies

### For Partnership Firm

**Required:**
- Partnership deed
- Firm registration certificate (if registered)
- Address proof of firm
- Partner's identity proof
- Authorized partner's photograph

### For Trust/Society/NGO

**Required:**
- Trust deed/Registration certificate
- Address proof of registered office
- Trustee's/Managing trustee's identity proof
- Resolution for PAN application
- Trustee's photograph

## Document Format Requirements

### Original vs Copies

For **Online Application**:
- Upload scanned copies of original documents
- Send self-attested photocopies by post with signed acknowledgment
- Originals not required to be sent

For **Offline Application**:
- Submit self-attested photocopies
- Originals for verification (returned immediately)

### Self-Attestation

- Sign on each photocopy
- Write "Self-attested" or "True copy"
- Date and sign

### Photograph Requirements

**Specifications:**
- Size: 3.5 cm √ó 2.5 cm (passport size)
- Recent (not older than 3 months)
- Colored photo preferred
- White or light-colored background
- Full face, front view
- No sunglasses or hat
- 2 copies required

**For Online Application:**
- Format: JPEG
- Size: 10 KB to 50 KB
- Dimensions: 200 pixels (width) √ó 230 pixels (height)

### Document Scan Quality

- **Format**: PDF or JPEG
- **Size**: Maximum 1 MB per document
- **Resolution**: 200 DPI minimum
- **Color**: Color scan preferred
- **Clarity**: All text should be clearly readable

## Special Cases

### If You Don't Have Aadhaar

Use combination of:
- Voter ID (for identity and address)
- Birth certificate or 10th certificate (for DOB)

### If Address on Documents Doesn't Match

**Option 1**: Update address on existing documents first
**Option 2**: Provide both documents (old and new address) with explanation letter
**Option 3**: Submit rent agreement + landlord's address proof

### For Rented Accommodation

**Documents Accepted:**
- Registered/notarized rent agreement
- Landlord's NOC with their address proof
- Electricity/water bill in your name
- Bank statement with current address

### Name Mismatch in Documents

**Solution:**
- Use documents with exact same name
- Or provide supporting document showing name variation (marriage certificate, gazette notification, affidavit)

## FAQs

**Q: Can I use photocopies downloaded from DigiLocker?**
A: Yes, DigiLocker documents are acceptable if properly downloaded and printed.

**Q: Is Aadhaar mandatory for PAN?**
A: Aadhaar is not mandatory for PAN application, but once you have PAN, linking it with Aadhaar is mandatory.

**Q: Can I use my father's address proof?**
A: Yes, if you're dependent and staying with parents, you can use parent's address proof with a declaration.

**Q: What if I don't have electricity bill in my name?**
A: Use rent agreement, bank statement, or any other acceptable address proof.

**Q: Can scanned signature be used?**
A: For online application, yes. But for offline, original signature required on form.
