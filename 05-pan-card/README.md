# PAN Card

PAN is an abbreviation for **Permanent Account Number**. This card is issued by the Income Tax Department to residents of India who are above 18 years.

## What is PAN Card?

PAN Card is a unique 10-character alphanumeric identifier issued by the Income Tax Department of India. It serves as:
- **Tax identification number**
- **Financial transaction identifier**
- **Identity proof**
- **Universal identification for financial transactions**

### PAN Card Format
The 10-character PAN is structured as follows:
- **First 3 characters**: Alphabets (sequence of letters)
- **4th character**: Status (P for Person, C for Company, H for HUF, etc.)
- **5th character**: First letter of name
- **Next 4 characters**: Numbers (0001 to 9999)
- **Last character**: Alphabetic check digit

**Example**: ABCDE1234F

## Who Issues PAN Cards?

**Income Tax Department** through:
- **NSDL (National Securities Depository Limited)**
- **UTIITSL (UTI Infrastructure Technology and Services Limited)**

These agencies are authorized by the Income Tax Department to process PAN applications.
