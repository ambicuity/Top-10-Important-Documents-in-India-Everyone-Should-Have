# Top 10 Important Documents in India ‚Äì Everyone Should Have
**By Ritesh Rana**

What documents prove Indian citizenship? Aadhar card, birth certificate, pan card, or what? This repository covers the top 10 important documents in India that every Indian must have to prove they are Indian and to work, study, or avail of the policies introduced by the Indian government.

You need documents to apply for a job, especially a government job. Without these documents, you cannot open a bank account, get admission to school or college, do business, get married, or shift to a foreign country.

## üìÑ List of Top 10 Important Documents

1. **[Ration Card](./01-ration-card)** - Subsidised food distribution document
2. **[Birth Certificate](./02-birth-certificate)** - Your first identity proof
3. **[Aadhaar Card](./03-aadhaar-card)** - 12-digit unique identification number
4. **[Educational Documents](./04-educational-documents)** - Academic certificates and degrees
5. **[PAN Card](./05-pan-card)** - Permanent Account Number for taxation
6. **[Passport](./06-passport)** - International travel document
7. **[Driving Licence](./07-driving-licence)** - Vehicle operation permit
8. **[Voter ID](./08-voter-id)** - Electoral identity card
9. **[Marriage Certificate](./09-marriage-certificate)** - Proof of marriage
10. **[Bank Passbook](./10-bank-passbook)** - Banking record document
