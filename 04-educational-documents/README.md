# Educational Documents

Educational certificates are something everyone needs to study and prove you have gained knowledge in a particular subject or area. Educational certificates also prove that you have completed your education at a specific level.

## What are Educational Documents?

Educational documents are official certificates, mark sheets, and degrees issued by recognized educational boards, universities, and institutions that certify your academic qualifications and achievements.

## Categories of Educational Documents

### School Level
- 10th Standard (Matriculation/SSLC)
- 12th Standard (Higher Secondary/Intermediate)
- School Leaving Certificate
- Transfer Certificate
- Character Certificate

### Undergraduate Level
- Bachelor's Degree (BA, B.Sc, B.Com, BBA, BCA, etc.)
- Engineering Degree (B.Tech, BE)
- Medical Degree (MBBS)
- Nursing Certificate/Degree
- Diploma Certificates
- Mark Sheets and Grade Cards

### Postgraduate Level
- Master's Degree (MA, M.Sc, M.Com, MBA, MCA, etc.)
- PG Diploma Certificates
- MD/MS Certificates (Medical)
- M.Tech/ME (Engineering)

### Professional and Specialized
- Ph.D. Certificates
- Professional Certifications
- Technical Training Certificates
- Vocational Training Certificates
- Online Course Certificates

## Authentication and Verification

Educational documents must be:
- **Authentic**: Issued by recognized institutions
- **Verified**: Can be verified with issuing authority
- **Attested**: For international use, require attestation
- **Preserved**: Should be kept safely as originals are irreplaceable
