# Complete List of Educational Certificates

## School Level Certificates

### 1. 10th Standard Certificate (Matriculation/SSLC)

**Full Names:**
- Matriculation Certificate
- SSLC (Secondary School Leaving Certificate)
- SSC (Secondary School Certificate)
- Class X Certificate

**Issued By:**
- Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE)
- State education boards
- ICSE (Indian Certificate of Secondary Education)
- NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling)

**Importance:**
- First major academic milestone
- Required for 11th standard admission
- Age proof document
- Minimum qualification for many jobs

### 2. 12th Standard Certificate (Higher Secondary)

**Full Names:**
- Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC)
- Senior Secondary Certificate
- Intermediate Certificate
- Class XII Certificate
- Pre-University Certificate (PUC)

**Issued By:**
- CBSE
- State boards (HSC boards)
- ICSE/ISC
- NIOS

**Importance:**
- Required for college/university admission
- Minimum qualification for many competitive exams
- Required for diploma and degree courses

### 3. 10th and 12th Mark Sheets

**Contents:**
- Subject-wise marks
- Total marks and percentage
- Grade/division
- Roll number and registration details
- Board seal and signature

**Importance:**
- Proof of academic performance
- Required for admissions (merit-based)
- Needed for scholarship applications

**Issued By:**

**Importance:**

### 5. Engineering Degree Certificate

**Types:**
- B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology)
- BE (Bachelor of Engineering)

**Specializations:**
- Computer Science
- Mechanical Engineering
- Electrical Engineering
- Civil Engineering
- Electronics & Communication
- And many more

**Issued By:**

**Importance:**

**Full Name:**
- MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)

**Issued By:**
- Medical colleges affiliated with universities
- Recognized by Medical Council of India (MCI) / National Medical Commission (NMC)

**Importance:**
- License to practice medicine
- Required for MD/MS postgraduate courses
- Essential for medical jobs in India and abroad
- Mandatory registration with state medical council

### 7. Nursing Certificate/Degree

**Issued By:**

**Importance:**
- Required for nursing jobs
- Essential for registration with nursing council
- Needed for hospital employment

## Format of Educational Certificates

Most educational certificates contain:

- Student's name
- Father's/Mother's name
- Date of birth
- Roll number/Registration number
- Name of the course/degree
- Subjects studied
- Year of passing
- Division/Grade/CGPA
- Institution/Board/University name
- Seal and signature of authority
- Date of issue

## Document Preservation

✅ Store originals in waterproof, fireproof safe
✅ Get multiple attested photocopies
✅ Scan and store digital copies in cloud
✅ Laminate mark sheets (but not degree certificates)
✅ Keep separate folders for each level
✅ Maintain chronological order

## Getting Duplicate Certificates

If original is lost:
1. File FIR (First Information Report) with police
2. Place advertisement in newspaper
3. Apply to issuing authority with:
   - Application for duplicate
   - FIR copy
   - Newspaper cutting
   - Affidavit
   - Fee payment (₹500-₹1000 typically)
4. Wait 30-60 days for duplicate issuance
