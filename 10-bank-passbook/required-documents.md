- **Passport**

- **Utility Bills** (electricity/water/gas - not older than 2 months)
- **Rent Agreement** (registered/notarized)
- **Bank statement** from another bank

### 3. PAN Card (Mandatory)

- Required for all bank accounts
- As per Income Tax regulations
- For interest income tax purposes

### 4. Photographs

- 2-3 passport-size photographs
- Recent (not older than 3 months)
- Colored preferred
- Same as used in other documents

### 5. Initial Deposit

- As per account type requirement
- Can be cash, cheque, or demand draft
- Zero for BSBD accounts

## Additional Requirements

### For Minors (Below 18 years)

**Minor's Documents**:
- Birth certificate (mandatory)
- School ID card
- Aadhaar card (if available)
- Photographs

**Parent/Guardian Documents**:
- All KYC documents (ID, address, PAN)
- Parent's photographs
- Relationship proof
- Parent's bank account details (some banks)

### For NRIs (Non-Resident Indians)

**NRE/NRO Account**:
- Passport (mandatory)
- Visa copy
- Overseas address proof
- Indian address proof (if available)
- PAN card (or Form 60 if no PAN)
- Employment proof abroad
- Photographs

### For Senior Citizens

- Same as regular account
- Age proof (any ID showing 60+ years)
- Senior citizen benefits form

### For Joint Account

**For Each Account Holder**:
- All KYC documents
- Photographs
- Signatures
- Joint account operating instructions

### For Company/Business Account

- Certificate of Incorporation
- PAN of company
- MOA and AOA
- Board resolution
- Authorized signatories' KYC
- GST registration (if applicable)
- Business address proof

## Special Cases

### If You Don't Have PAN Card

- Can still open account
- Must submit Form 60/61 (declaration)
- PAN application receipt (if applied)
- Must get PAN within specified time

### If Address Proof is Different from Current Address

- Submit available address proof
- Provide declaration of current address
- Bank may conduct address verification

### For Rented Accommodation

- Rent agreement (registered/notarized)
- Utility bill in your name
- Landlord's NOC with their address proof
