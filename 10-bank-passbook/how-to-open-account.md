# How to Open a Bank Account

## Types of Bank Accounts

1. **Savings Account** - For individuals
2. **Current Account** - For businesses
3. **Salary Account** - For salaried employees
4. **Senior Citizen Account** - For people above 60 years
5. **Minor Account** - For children below 18 years
6. **No Frills Account/Basic Savings Bank Deposit (BSBD)** - Zero balance account

## Methods to Open Bank Account

1. **Visit Bank Branch** (Traditional method)
2. **Online Account Opening** (Video KYC)
3. **Mobile Banking App**

## Method 1: Visit Bank Branch

### Step 1: Choose a Bank

Consider:
- Bank reputation
- Branch proximity
- Minimum balance requirements
- Interest rates
- Online banking facilities
- Customer service

### Step 2: Collect Account Opening Form

- Visit nearest branch
- Request savings account form
- Or download from bank website

### Step 3: Fill Application Form

Provide:
- Personal details (name, DOB, father's name)
- Contact information (mobile, email, address)
- Occupation details
- Nominee details (optional but recommended)
- Initial deposit amount
- Type of account

### Step 4: Submit KYC Documents

**Required Documents**:
- Identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Voter ID/Passport)
- Address proof (Aadhaar/utility bill/rent agreement)
- PAN card (mandatory)
- Photographs (2-3 passport size)

### Step 5: Initial Deposit

- Minimum amount varies by account type
- Can be cash, cheque, or DD
- Zero balance for BSBD accounts

### Step 6: Verification

- Bank verifies documents
- May conduct address verification
- Takes 1-7 days

### Step 7: Account Activation

- Account number issued
- Welcome kit provided
- Passbook, cheque book, debit card issued

## Method 2: Online Account Opening (Video KYC)

### Step 1: Visit Bank Website

- Go to bank's official website
- Look for "Open Account Online"

### Step 2: Fill Online Application

- Provide personal details
- Upload documents
- Aadhaar-based eKYC
- PAN card details

### Step 3: Video KYC

- Schedule video call with bank representative
- Show original documents to camera
- Verification done in real-time
- Signature captured digitally

### Step 4: Initial Deposit

- Fund via net banking/UPI/NEFT
- Or visit branch later

### Step 5: Account Activation

- Account activated instantly or within 24 hours
- Details sent via email/SMS
- Digital welcome kit

## After Account Opening

1. **Activate Debit Card**: Set PIN at ATM
2. **Register for Net Banking**: Visit website or branch
3. **Register for Mobile Banking**: Download app
4. **Link Aadhaar**: Mandatory (can do online/at branch)
5. **Set Nominee**: If not done during opening
6. **Start Using**: Deposit/withdraw as needed
