- **Purpose**: Central portal for National Food Security Act information

## Grievance Redressal

### National Helpline
- **Number**: 1800-11-0841
- **Email**: support@nfsa.gov.in
- **Timing**: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (Monday to Friday)

### Online Complaint Portal
- **Website**: [https://pgportal.gov.in](https://pgportal.gov.in)
- **Purpose**: Register and track complaints

## Track Your Application

Most state portals provide application tracking:
1. Visit your state portal
2. Look for "Track Application Status"
3. Enter application reference number
4. View current status

## Download Forms

### Application Forms
- Available on respective state portals
- Can be downloaded in PDF format
- Also available at local food supply offices

## Mobile Apps

Several states have launched mobile apps for ration card services:
- **Delhi**: NFSA Delhi App
- **Karnataka**: AHARA App
- **Tamil Nadu**: TNPDS App
- **Maharashtra**: Mahafood App

Search for your state-specific app on Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

## Useful Resources

### One Nation One Ration Card (ONORC)
- **Website**: [https://nfsa.gov.in/portal/ONORC_laden](https://nfsa.gov.in/portal/ONORC_laden)
- **Purpose**: Use ration card across India

### Check Ration Card Details
Most states allow checking ration card details online using:
- Ration card number
- Aadhaar number
- Family member name

## Social Media

Stay updated through official social media handles of your state's Food & Civil Supplies Department.

## Important Updates

Always check official portals for:
- Scheme announcements
- Document requirement changes
- Application deadlines
- New features and services

## Contact Information

For specific queries:
1. Visit local food supply office
2. Call state helpline
3. Send email to state department
4. Use online grievance portal

## Note

⚠️ **Beware of Frauds**: Always use official government websites. Don't pay unauthorized agents for ration card services.
