# How to Apply for Aadhaar Card

## Eligibility

- Indian resident (living in India for at least 182 days in the last 12 months)
- No age limit (from newborn to elderly)
- One Aadhaar per person (no duplicates allowed)

## Methods to Apply

### 1. Visit Aadhaar Enrollment Center
### 2. Book Online Appointment
### 3. Aadhaar Sewa Kendra

## Method 1: Visit Aadhaar Enrollment Center (Walk-in)

### Step 1: Locate Nearest Center
- Visit [https://appointments.uidai.gov.in/easearch.aspx](https://appointments.uidai.gov.in/easearch.aspx)
- Enter your state, district, and sub-district
- Get list of nearby enrollment centers with addresses

### Step 2: Visit the Center
- Carry original documents (see required documents list)
- Visit during working hours (usually 10 AM to 5 PM)
- No need for appointment (but may have longer wait time)

### Step 3: Fill Enrollment Form
- Operator will help you fill the form
- Or fill manually if form is provided
- Provide accurate information:
  - Full name (as per documents)
  - Date of birth
  - Gender
  - Mobile number
  - Email address
  - Address details

### Step 4: Submit Documents
- Submit original documents for verification
- Operator will verify and return originals
- Documents will be scanned/photocopied

### Step 5: Biometric Capture
- **Fingerprints**: All 10 fingers
- **Iris Scan**: Both eyes
- **Photograph**: Passport-style photo
- **Signature/Thumb impression**

**Note**: Children below 5 years - only photograph, no biometrics

### Step 6: Verify Details
- Operator will show all entered information on screen
- Verify carefully:
  - Name spelling
  - Date of birth
  - Address
  - Mobile number
  - Email

### Step 7: Get Acknowledgment Slip
- Receive enrollment slip with:
  - 14-digit Enrollment ID (EID)
  - Date and time of enrollment
  - Details of documents submitted
- Keep this slip safe - needed for tracking

### Step 8: Track Status
- Wait 90 days for processing
- Track status using EID on UIDAI website
- Receive SMS update on registered mobile

### Step 9: Download e-Aadhaar
- Once generated, download from UIDAI website
- Use enrollment ID or Aadhaar number
- Enter registered mobile/email for OTP
- Password for PDF: Combination of name and birth year

## Method 2: Online Appointment Booking

### Step 1: Visit UIDAI Appointment Portal
**Website**: [https://appointments.uidai.gov.in](https://appointments.uidai.gov.in)

### Step 2: Click on "Book Appointment"
- Select your state
- Select district and sub-district
- Choose enrollment center from list

### Step 3: Select Date and Time
- View available slots
- Choose convenient date and time
- Limited slots available per day

### Step 4: Fill Personal Details
- Enter name, mobile number, email
- Provide basic information
- Create appointment

### Step 5: Receive Confirmation
- SMS and email confirmation sent
- Note appointment time and center address
- Print/save appointment confirmation

### Step 6: Visit Center on Scheduled Time
- Reach 10-15 minutes early
- Show appointment confirmation
- Follow steps 3-9 from Method 1

## For Children (Below 5 Years)

### Baal Aadhaar Enrollment

**Documents Required**:
- Any one document of parents (Aadhaar/Passport/Birth Certificate)
- Child's birth certificate
- One parent must be present

**Process**:
1. Visit enrollment center with child
2. Only photograph is captured (no biometrics)
3. Parent's biometric is captured for verification
4. Blue-colored Baal Aadhaar issued
5. Must update at age 5 with biometrics
6. Must update again at age 15 with biometrics

## For Children (5-18 Years)

**Documents Required**:
- Birth certificate
- School ID card with photograph
- One parent's Aadhaar card

**Process**:
- Full biometric enrollment (fingerprints + iris + photo)
- Parent/guardian must accompany
- Mandatory update at age 15

## Aadhaar Enrollment Fees

**FREE** - No charges for:
- New enrollment
- Biometric update (for children at 5 and 15 years)
- Demographic update

**Charged Services**:
- Reprint of Aadhaar (if lost): ₹50
- Change in demographic details: Free (first time), ₹50 (subsequent)

## Processing Time

- **Enrollment to Generation**: 60-90 days
- **Status Update**: Updated weekly
- **SMS Notification**: Sent upon generation
- **e-Aadhaar**: Available immediately after generation

## Types of Aadhaar

### 1. Physical Aadhaar Card
- Sent by post to registered address
- May take 30-60 days to arrive
- Not mandatory to wait for physical card

### 2. e-Aadhaar (Digital)
- Downloadable PDF from UIDAI website
- Password protected
- Legally valid everywhere
- Can be printed on plain paper

### 3. m-Aadhaar (Mobile App)
- Download "m-Aadhaar" app from Google Play/App Store
- Digital Aadhaar on mobile
- QR code based
- Accepted at most places

### 4. PVC Aadhaar Card
- **New service**: Order PVC card online
- **Fee**: ₹50
- **Delivery**: 5-7 days by speed post
- **Durable**: Water and tear resistant
- **Order from**: [https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in](https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in)

## Tracking Your Aadhaar Application

### Online Tracking

**Website**: [https://uidai.gov.in/en/my-aadhaar/check-aadhaar-status.html](https://uidai.gov.in/en/my-aadhaar/check-aadhaar-status.html)

**Steps**:
1. Enter 14-digit Enrollment ID (from acknowledgment slip)
2. Enter 14-digit date and time stamp
3. Enter security code (captcha)
4. Click "Check Status"

**Status Messages**:
- "Aadhaar is not yet generated" - Processing in progress
- "Aadhaar has been generated" - Ready to download
- "Aadhaar has been dispatched" - Physical card sent by post

### By SMS

**For Mobile**:
- Send SMS: `GETSTATUS <14-digit EID>` to 51969
- Example: GETSTATUS 12345678901234

## Download e-Aadhaar

### Method 1: Using Aadhaar Number

**Website**: [https://eaadhaar.uidai.gov.in](https://eaadhaar.uidai.gov.in)

1. Click on "Download Aadhaar"
2. Enter 12-digit Aadhaar number
3. Enter security code
4. Get OTP on registered mobile
5. Enter OTP
6. Download e-Aadhaar PDF
7. **Password**: First 4 letters of name (capital) + Year of birth
   - Example: If name is "Rahul Kumar" and DOB is 15/08/1990, password is "RAHU1990"

### Method 2: Using Enrollment ID

1. Select "Enrolment ID" option
2. Enter 14-digit EID
3. Enter 14-digit date-time stamp
4. Follow OTP process (as above)

### Method 3: Using Virtual ID (VID)

1. Generate VID first from UIDAI portal
2. Use VID instead of Aadhaar number
3. Follow same download process

## Aadhaar for NRIs

**Eligibility**: Must have stayed in India for 182 days or more in the last 12 months

**Documents Accepted**:
- Passport
- Visa documents showing Indian visit dates
- Any Indian address proof

**Process**: Same as regular enrollment

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Issue 1: Biometric Not Captured
**Solution**: Visit enrollment center for biometric update (free)

### Issue 2: Mobile Number Not Updated
**Solution**: Visit Aadhaar center with documents to update mobile

### Issue 3: Wrong Information in Aadhaar
**Solution**: Apply for correction online or visit center

### Issue 4: Aadhaar Not Generated After 90 Days
**Solution**: 
- Check status online
- Contact UIDAI helpline: 1947
- Visit enrollment center with acknowledgment slip

## Important Points to Remember

✅ Enrollment is **completely FREE**
✅ No agent or middleman required
✅ Keep acknowledgment slip safe
✅ Verify all details before confirming
✅ Provide correct mobile number for updates
✅ e-Aadhaar is legally valid (no need to wait for physical card)
✅ Update Aadhaar within 10 years if no changes
✅ Link mobile number for OTP-based services
